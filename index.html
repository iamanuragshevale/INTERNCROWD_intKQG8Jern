Welcome To Intern Crowd LLP


An engineering intern is taken on by a company for on-the-job learning of engineering skills. Engineering interns tend to be recent university graduates with degrees in engineering who are seeking relevant real-world experience in the workplace. Engineering interns tend to work alongside a company's employees, assisting them and learning what they do. They may be given tasks to accomplish that are necessary but which don't require a fully qualified person to do.
